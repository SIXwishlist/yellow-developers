Connect with other Yellow users and find professional help.

## How to get support

* [Report problems and suggestions](https://github.com/datenstrom/yellow/issues)
* [Chat with the developers](https://gitter.im/datenstrom/yellow)
* [Contact a human](http://datenstrom.se/contact/)

## Where to find us

* [Twitter @datenstromse](https://twitter.com/datenstromse)
* [GitHub @datenstrom](https://github.com/datenstrom)
* [Gitter @datenstrom](https://gitter.im/datenstrom/yellow)

## How to contribute

* [Review new suggestions](https://github.com/datenstrom/yellow/issues?q=is%3Aopen+is%3Aissue+label%3Asuggestion)
* [Create a translation](https://github.com/datenstrom/yellow-plugins/tree/master/language)
* [Create plugins](https://github.com/datenstrom/yellow-plugins)
* [Create themes](https://github.com/datenstrom/yellow-themes)

## Thank you

Yellow is free and open source, made by the people who use Yellow. At the heart of open source is the idea that by sharing code, we can make better software together. Contributions and feedback are very welcome. Licensed under [GPLv2](http://opensource.org/licenses/GPL-2.0).

Big thanks to [core maintainers](https://github.com/datenstrom/yellow/graphs/contributors) and [contributors](https://github.com/datenstrom/yellow-plugins/graphs/contributors).
