Datenstrom Yellow is for people who make websites. Create small web pages, blogs and wikis. The focus is on people and that it's useful for you. No database, no admin panel, no framework. Most people don't need all of this. If you are interested in simple things, find out more. [--more--]

* Fun to use
* Just files and folders
* Installed in a few seconds
