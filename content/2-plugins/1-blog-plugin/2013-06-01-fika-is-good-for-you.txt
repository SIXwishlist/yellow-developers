Fika is a Swedish custom. It's a social coffee break where people gather to have a cup of coffee or tea together. You can have fika with colleagues at work. You can invite your friends to fika. Fika is such an important part of life in Sweden that it is both a verb and a noun. Do you fika? [--more--]
