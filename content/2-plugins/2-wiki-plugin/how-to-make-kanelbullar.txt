Kanelbullar are a Swedish bakery also known as cinnamon buns.

1. Start with a regular yeast dough from 700g flour, 100g sugar, 400ml milk, 40g butter, 2 teaspoons salt, 1 teaspoon cardamon and a package of yeast. Let it rise for 40 minutes, then role out thin on a flat surface.  
2. Spread 40g butter, 60g sugar and 1 tablespoon cinnamon on dough. Role and cut into 12 equal pieces. Let them rise for 40 minutes on a backing sheet. Add egg wash and sprinkle with pearl sugar. Bake in oven at 200ÂºC for 14 minutes.
3. Enjoy.
